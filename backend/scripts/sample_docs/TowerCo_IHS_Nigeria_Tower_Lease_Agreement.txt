3. CONTRACT TERMS AND CONDITIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1  Lease Term
     This Agreement shall commence on 1 July 2024 and expire on 30 June
     2026, unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 9 or
     renewed pursuant to Section 3.3 below.

3.2  Monthly Lease Fee
     The monthly lease fee payable by MTN Nigeria to IHS Nigeria for all
     six (6) sites shall be NGN 28,000,000 (Twenty-Eight Million Naira),
     exclusive of applicable taxes. Payment is due on the 1st business day
     of each calendar month.

3.3  Renewal
     Either party may initiate renewal negotiations no later than ninety
     (90) calendar days before the Expiry Date (i.e., no later than
     1 April 2026). Failure to serve written renewal notice by 1 April
     2026 shall result in automatic expiry of this Agreement on 30 June
     2026 without further obligation by either party.

3.4  Annual Fee Escalation
     The monthly lease fee shall increase by 10% per annum on each
     anniversary of the Commencement Date, effective 1 July 2025.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
4. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1  Uptime Guarantee
     IHS Nigeria guarantees a minimum aggregate uptime of 99.5% per
     calendar month across all six (6) leased sites. Uptime is measured
     from the first day to the last day of each calendar month.

4.2  Penalty for SLA Breach
     In the event that aggregate uptime in any calendar month falls below
     99.5%, IHS Nigeria shall apply a fee reduction to the following
     month's invoice at the rate of:

         2% (two percent) of the monthly lease fee per 0.1% (zero point
         one percent) by which actual uptime is below the 99.5% threshold.

     Example: Uptime of 99.3% = 0.2% shortfall = 4% fee reduction.
              Fee reduction = 4% × NGN 28,000,000 = NGN 1,120,000.

4.3  Measurement and Reporting
     IHS Nigeria shall provide a monthly uptime report to MTN Nigeria
     by the 5th business day of the following month. MTN Nigeria may
     dispute reported uptime within 10 business days of receipt.

5. MAINTENANCE OBLIGATIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5.1  IHS Nigeria shall carry out preventive maintenance on each leased
     site on a quarterly basis. Maintenance records shall be provided to
     MTN Nigeria within 5 business days of each maintenance visit and
     entered into the MTN Nigeria change-management system.

5.2  IHS Nigeria shall maintain each site's diesel generator and automatic
     transfer switch (ATS) in full working order at all times. Any ATS
     relay with more than 4 years of operational service shall be replaced
     during the next scheduled quarterly maintenance visit.

5.3  MTN Nigeria may commission independent audits of maintenance records
     with 48 hours' written notice to IHS Nigeria.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
6. POWER AND UTILITIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

6.1  IHS Nigeria is responsible for utility power procurement and diesel
     supply for each leased site. Utility power failures beyond IHS
     Nigeria's control do not excuse SLA obligations where the failure
     exceeds the generator autonomy period (72 hours of diesel supply).

6.2  IHS Nigeria shall maintain a minimum 72-hour diesel reserve at each
     site at all times.

7. TERMINATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

7.1  Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days' written
     notice for cause, including but not limited to: material breach of
     SLA obligations (defined as three (3) consecutive months below the
     99.5% uptime threshold), insolvency, or regulatory action.

7.2  MTN Nigeria may terminate individual site leases with 30 days'
     written notice in the event of planned network decommissioning or
     technology upgrade requiring site vacating.
