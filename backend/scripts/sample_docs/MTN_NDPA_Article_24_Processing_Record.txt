1. PURPOSE AND LEGAL BASIS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1.1  This record is maintained pursuant to Article 24 of the Nigeria Data
     Protection Act 2023 (NDPA), which requires every data controller of
     major importance to maintain a record of all processing activities.

1.2  MTN Nigeria Communications Plc is classified as a Data Controller of
     Major Importance under the NDPA by virtue of its subscriber base
     exceeding 1 million data subjects and its operation of critical
     communications infrastructure.

1.3  Legal basis for processing: Contractual necessity (Article 25(2)(b)),
     Legitimate interests (Article 25(2)(f)), and Legal obligation
     (Article 25(2)(c)) as applicable to each processing purpose described
     in Section 2.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. PROCESSING PURPOSES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1  Subscriber Onboarding and Identity Verification
     Purpose      : Verify subscriber identity per NCC SIM registration
                    requirements; open service account.
     Legal Basis  : Legal obligation (NCC Regulations) and contractual.
     Data Used    : National ID number, BVN, name, address, biometrics.

2.2  Service Delivery and Network Operations
     Purpose      : Provide telecommunications services (voice, data, SMS);
                    operate and maintain network infrastructure.
     Legal Basis  : Contractual necessity.
     Data Used    : MSISDN, IMSI, IMEI, call/data records, location data.

2.3  Billing and Payment Processing
     Purpose      : Generate invoices; process airtime and MoMo wallet
                    transactions; manage credit and debt.
     Legal Basis  : Contractual necessity.
     Data Used    : Call records, data usage, payment card details,
                    MoMo wallet transaction logs.

2.4  Fraud Prevention and Security
     Purpose      : Detect and prevent fraudulent transactions; protect
                    subscriber accounts; comply with AML obligations.
     Legal Basis  : Legitimate interests; legal obligation.
     Data Used    : Transaction patterns, device identifiers, location,
                    call patterns.

2.5  Regulatory Reporting
     Purpose      : Submit QoS returns, lawful intercept, and other
                    mandatory NCC and government filings.
     Legal Basis  : Legal obligation.
     Data Used    : Aggregate network data, subscriber records as required
                    by court order or regulatory direction.

2.6  Marketing and Customer Retention
     Purpose      : Send promotional offers; personalise service
                    recommendations.
     Legal Basis  : Consent (where obtained); legitimate interests.
     Data Used    : Usage patterns, demographics, location (aggregated).
     Note         : Opt-out mechanism available via *123# and MTN app.

3. DATA CATEGORIES AND RETENTION PERIODS

Category                    Retention Period    Basis
——————————————————————————  ——————————————————  ——————————————————————
Subscriber identity data    5 years post-churn  NCC SIM Regs
Call detail records (CDR)   5 years             MTN Policy v3.2 Sec 4.2
Data usage logs             5 years             MTN Policy v3.2 Sec 4.2
Billing records             7 years             FIRS / Tax obligations
MoMo transaction logs       7 years             CBN regulations
Fraud investigation records 7 years             EFCC / Legal hold
Network performance logs    5 years*            MTN Policy v3.2 Sec 4.2

* NOTE: NCC Consumer Protection Regulations 2023 require network
  performance logs to be retained for 7 years. MTN Policy v3.2 specifies
  5 years. A policy update (v3.3) is in progress. Interim preservation
  extended to 7 years pending formal policy amendment. DPO to confirm.
