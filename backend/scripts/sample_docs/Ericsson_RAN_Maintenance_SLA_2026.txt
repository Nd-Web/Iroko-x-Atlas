1. PARTIES AND BACKGROUND
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1.1  Service Provider: Ericsson Nigeria Limited, RC 123456, 3 Kofo Abayomi
     Street, Victoria Island, Lagos, Nigeria ("Ericsson").

1.2  Customer: MTN Nigeria Communications Plc, RC 395010, MTN Plaza,
     Falomo, Ikoyi, Lagos, Nigeria ("MTN Nigeria").

1.3  This Agreement governs the provision of corrective and preventive
     maintenance services for MTN Nigeria's Radio Access Network (RAN)
     equipment portfolio as described in Schedule 1.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1  Covered Equipment
     This Agreement covers maintenance services for 847 (eight hundred and
     forty-seven) base stations nationwide as listed in Schedule 1 (Site
     Register). The Site Register shall be updated by mutual agreement
     within 30 days of any network expansion or decommissioning activity.

2.2  Corrective Maintenance
     Ericsson shall provide corrective maintenance services (fault diagnosis,
     repair, and restoration) for all covered base stations in response to
     fault notifications raised by MTN Nigeria's NOC.

2.3  Preventive Maintenance
     Ericsson shall perform scheduled preventive maintenance visits to all
     covered base stations on a bi-annual basis (every 6 months). A
     preventive maintenance calendar for 2026 is attached as Schedule 2.

2.4  Spare Parts Availability
     Ericsson shall maintain a critical spares inventory at its Lagos Depot
     sufficient to guarantee availability of 95% of critical RAN spare
     parts within 2 hours of fault notification for Lagos sites, and 8 hours
     for all other regions.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
3. SERVICE LEVEL COMMITMENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1  Response Time SLA

     Fault Priority   Definition                    Response Time   Resolution Time
     —————————————    ————————————————————————————  —————————————   ———————————————
     Critical (P1)    Full site outage or full       4 hours         24 hours
                      cluster outage
     High (P2)        Sector outage (≥ 50% capacity) 8 hours         48 hours
     Medium (P3)      Degraded performance           24 hours        5 business days
     Low (P4)         Non-service-affecting          72 hours        15 business days

3.2  Response time is measured from the time MTN Nigeria logs a fault in
     the ServiceNow system (as notified to Ericsson by automated alert)
     to the time an Ericsson field engineer arrives on-site.

3.3  Resolution time is measured from the time of response to the time
     the affected site is restored to full service.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
4. COMMERCIAL TERMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1  Monthly Service Fee
     The monthly fee payable by MTN Nigeria to Ericsson for services under
     this Agreement shall be NGN 15,000,000 (Fifteen Million Naira),
     exclusive of applicable taxes. Payment is due on the 5th business day
     of each calendar month.

4.2  Annual Value
     Total annual contract value: NGN 180,000,000 (One Hundred and Eighty
     Million Naira).

4.3  Fee Escalation
     The monthly fee shall increase by 8% per annum on each anniversary
     of the Commencement Date.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
5. SLA BREACH AND CREDITS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5.1  In the event of a response time SLA breach for a P1 fault, Ericsson
     shall apply a service credit to the following month's invoice per the
     schedule in Schedule B of this Agreement.

5.2  Schedule B (Credit Schedule — P1 Response Breach):
     — Breach by up to 30 minutes  : NGN 50,000 credit per occurrence
     — Breach by 30–60 minutes     : NGN 150,000 credit per occurrence
     — Breach by over 60 minutes   : NGN 300,000 credit per occurrence

5.3  Total credits in any calendar month shall not exceed 20% of the
     monthly service fee.

5.4  Note: Incident INC-2026-IKJ-0147 (14 February 2026) — Ericsson
     response time was 4 hours 12 minutes (12-minute breach of 4-hour P1
     SLA). Credit of NGN 50,000 is applicable per Schedule B.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
6. PARTS AND LOGISTICS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

6.1  Ericsson shall maintain a Lagos parts depot stocked with all critical
     RAN spare parts. Parts availability guarantee: 95% of critical spares
     available within 2 hours for Lagos sites.

6.2  For sites outside Lagos, Ericsson shall maintain regional depots in
     Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Kano with 8-hour parts availability SLA.

6.3  All parts used under this Agreement must be genuine Ericsson-certified
     components. No third-party or grey-market parts shall be used.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
7. REPORTING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

7.1  Ericsson shall provide a monthly maintenance report to MTN Nigeria
     by the 5th business day of the following month, covering:
     — All P1 and P2 faults attended
     — Response and resolution times vs SLA
     — Preventive maintenance visits completed
     — Parts usage and depot stock levels

7.2  Monthly service review meetings shall be held on the 3rd Tuesday
     of each month between Ericsson account management and MTN Nigeria
     Network Operations.

8. TERM AND RENEWAL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

8.1  This Agreement expires on 31 December 2026. Renewal discussions
     should commence no later than 30 September 2026 (3 months before
     expiry).

8.2  Either party may terminate with 60 days' written notice for cause,
     including persistent SLA failure (defined as 3 consecutive months
     with more than 5% of P1 faults breaching response SLA).
