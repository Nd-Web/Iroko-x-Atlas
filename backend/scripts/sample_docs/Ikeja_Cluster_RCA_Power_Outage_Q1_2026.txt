1. INCIDENT SUMMARY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

On 14 February 2026 at 02:14 WAT, the Ikeja base station cluster experienced
a full power outage resulting in degradation of service across six (6) base
stations. Tower 4471 serves as the anchor site for the Ikeja cluster and was
the primary site affected. The incident lasted 4.2 hours, from 02:14 WAT to
06:23 WAT, impacting approximately 18,000 subscribers during the off-peak
window.

Incident Severity  : P1 — Critical
Affected Sites     : IKJ-001, IKJ-002, IKJ-003, IKJ-004, IKJ-005, IKJ-006
Anchor Site        : Tower 4471 (IKJ-001), Ikeja, Lagos State
Total Downtime     : 4.2 hours (252 minutes)

2. TIMELINE OF EVENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

02:14 WAT  — AES utility power failure on feeder supplying Ikeja cluster.
              Generator auto-transfer relay on IKJ-001 (Tower 4471) fails to
              activate. Sites IKJ-001 through IKJ-006 lose power within 90
              seconds.

02:19 WAT  — NOC NMS system raises P1 alarm: "Ikeja cluster — full site
              outage". On-call engineer notified via automated page.

02:31 WAT  — On-call engineer acknowledges alarm. Confirms 6-site outage.
              ServiceNow ticket INC-2026-IKJ-0147 raised.

02:45 WAT  — Field maintenance team dispatched to Tower 4471 (IKJ-001).
              Ericsson on-call team notified per RAN Maintenance SLA
              (ERIC/MTN/RAN/2026-001). Response SLA: 4 hours.

03:10 WAT  — Field team arrives at Tower 4471. Diagnoses generator
              auto-transfer relay fault. Requests replacement relay unit
              from Ericsson parts depot.

05:50 WAT  — Replacement relay unit delivered from Ericsson Lagos depot.

06:10 WAT  — Generator auto-transfer relay replaced and tested successfully.

06:23 WAT  — All 6 base stations restored to service. IHS Nigeria tower
              lease site IHS/MTN/IKJ/2024-001 uptime clock resumes.

06:30 WAT  — NOC confirms full service restoration. ServiceNow ticket
              INC-2026-IKJ-0147 resolved.

3. ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1 Primary Root Cause
    Failure of the generator automatic transfer switch (ATS) relay at Tower
    4471 (IKJ-001). When the AES utility feeder failed, the ATS did not
    transfer the load to the on-site diesel generator within the specified
    10-second window. The relay had not been replaced since installation in
    2021 and showed signs of mechanical wear beyond its rated service life.

3.2 Contributing Factors
    (a) IHS Nigeria tower lease (IHS/MTN/IKJ/2024-001) Schedule 4 requires
        quarterly preventive maintenance of ATS relays. The last recorded
        maintenance was September 2025 (Q3). The Q4 2025 maintenance visit
        was not completed — no record in the change-management log.
    (b) Ericsson RAN Maintenance SLA (ERIC/MTN/RAN/2026-001) specifies a
        4-hour critical fault response. Actual response time for relay
        delivery was 4 hours 12 minutes — a breach of 12 minutes. Ericsson
        has been notified.
    (c) No backup power interconnect between IKJ-001 and IKJ-002 to allow
        partial cluster continuity during single-site generator failure.

3.3 Why It Was Not Caught Earlier
    The Q4 2025 IHS Nigeria preventive maintenance record was not entered
    into the change-management log. This allowed the degraded relay to
    remain in service without triggering a replacement alert.

4. SLA IMPACT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 IHS Nigeria Tower Lease SLA
    Contracted uptime: 99.5% per calendar month.
    February 2026 actual uptime (Tower 4471): 99.42%.
    Breach: 0.08% below contracted SLA.
    Applicable penalty: 2% monthly lease fee reduction per 0.1% shortfall.
    Estimated penalty: NGN 560,000 (2% × NGN 28,000,000 monthly fee).

4.2 Enterprise Customer SLA Exposure
    Per the Enterprise Customer SLA Register — EBU (MTN-EBU-SLA-REG-2026),
    three (3) Tier-1 enterprise customers with Ikeja cluster connectivity
    are entitled to SLA credits.
    Credit formula: 10% of monthly contract value per hour of downtime.
    Total enterprise SLA credit exposure: NGN 2,100,000.
    Affected customers: Zenith Bank, NNPC, Dangote Group.

4.3 Ericsson RAN SLA
    Response SLA: 4 hours for critical faults.
    Actual response: 4 hours 12 minutes.
    Breach: 12 minutes. Ericsson escalation credit applicable per Schedule B
    of ERIC/MTN/RAN/2026-001.
