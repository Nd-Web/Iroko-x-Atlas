2. SCOPE OF WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1  The scope of works includes the full design, procurement, civil
     works, installation, and commissioning of a 287 km fibre optic cable
     route from Kano to Kaduna, including:

     (a) Fibre optic cable — 288-core single-mode fibre (G.652D standard)
     (b) Duct installation — HDPE microduct system, 2× 40mm ducts
     (c) Civil works — directional drilling at major road crossings;
         open trenching in rural sections
     (d) 12 POP shelter constructions along the route
     (e) Optical equipment installation and commissioning at each POP
     (f) Power systems — solar + battery backup at each POP
     (g) Fibre acceptance testing — OTDR and power budget validation
