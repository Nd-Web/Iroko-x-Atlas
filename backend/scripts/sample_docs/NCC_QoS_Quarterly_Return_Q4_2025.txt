7.1  All data and records underpinning this QoS return are retained in
     compliance with NCC Regulation requirements.

7.2  Per NCC Consumer Protection Regulations 2023, all QoS data and
     supporting network logs must be retained for a minimum of seven (7)
     years from the date of this submission (i.e., until 13 February 2033).

7.3  MTN Nigeria's current Data Retention Policy (v3.2, Section 4.2)
     specifies a 5-year retention period for operational network data.
     A policy update is in progress to align with the 7-year NCC requirement.
     Regulatory Affairs has confirmed interim data preservation measures are
     in place pending formal policy update.
