1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

MTN Nigeria received 2,847 customer complaints related to MoMo wallet
deductions during Q1 2026, representing a 187% increase over Q4 2025
(988 complaints). Total disputed transaction value: NGN 45,000,000.

Resolution rate as at report date: 73% (2,079 complaints resolved;
NGN 32,800,000 refunded). 768 complaints remain open.

The primary driver is unauthorised deduction complaints linked to the
Ikeja cluster power outage on 14 February 2026 (Incident INC-2026-IKJ-0147),
where MoMo transaction retries during network reconnection generated
duplicate deductions for approximately 1,730 affected subscribers.

2.3  Top Complaint Category: Unauthorised Deductions
     Of the 1,736 unauthorised deduction complaints, 1,730 (99.7%) occurred
     between 06:23 WAT and 09:00 WAT on 14 February 2026 — the period
     immediately following restoration of the Ikeja cluster (see Section 4).
     The MoMo platform's 30-second idempotency window failed to detect
     retries triggered after the 4.2-hour outage gap.

4. ROOT CAUSE — IKEJA OUTAGE LINKAGE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1  The Ikeja base station cluster power outage (02:14–06:23 WAT,
     14 February 2026) caused active MoMo sessions to be interrupted.
     When connectivity was restored at 06:23 WAT, the MoMo client
     applications on affected subscriber handsets automatically retried
     pending transactions.

4.2  The MoMo platform's idempotency key expiry window is set at 30
     seconds. Retries arriving 4.2 hours after the original transaction
     were treated as new transactions by the platform, resulting in
     duplicate deductions.

4.3  Corrective action required: extend the MoMo idempotency window to
     a minimum of 6 hours to cover extended outage reconnection gaps.
     This has been escalated to the MoMo Technology team (ticket:
     MOMO-TECH-2026-0341).

6. RECOMMENDED ACTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

6.1  Expedite resolution of 768 open complaints to avoid NCC penalties.
     Internal deadline: 30 April 2026.

6.2  Deploy MoMo idempotency window patch (MOMO-TECH-2026-0341) by
     30 April 2026. Testing in UAT environment completed 10 April 2026.

6.3  Proactively notify all 1,730 affected Ikeja subscribers of the
     duplicate deduction and offer automatic refund within 48 hours of
     patch deployment.

6.4  File incident linkage report connecting INC-2026-IKJ-0147 and
     CX-COMPLAINT-MOMO-Q1-2026 for regulatory audit trail.
